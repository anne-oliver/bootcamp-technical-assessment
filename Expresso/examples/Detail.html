Mocha ⭐️

27 minutes ago

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 cup of espresso or coffee
  • 0.25 cups of milk

Steps

  1. In a small pot, heated over a stove, stir together sugar, cocoa powder and water until a smooth mixture is formed.
  2. Stir in milk and coffee and heat until steaming. Whisk to create a foam, if desired
  3. Pour into a cup and optionally top with whipped cream.
